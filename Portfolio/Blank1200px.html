About Me

Alan Chou – an independent artist with a name that’s as cool as it sounds, except when my “unique” pronunciation gets it mistaken for “Adam.” Born and raised in Taiwan, I’ve always had a passion for music production, even if mixing and mastering sometimes feel like my ears are running a never-ending marathon. But hey, the further I go, the better I get. Yes, I create my own music, too—
ear damage is all part of the process, right? To keep sharpening my skills (or just to avoid my neighbors' complaints), I crossed the Pacific to the U.S., where I'm now pursuing my master's at Illinois State University’s CTK program. What inspires me? The smooth beat from Khalil Fong, the silky R&B vocals of David Tao, and Jacob Collier’s brain-bending compositions. It's like throwing the best musical party, and somehow, they all show up. I believe music has the power to turn even the dullest moments into a dance party (although I can’t dance at all). P.S.: Dad jokes are the best
